About Me

I am a junior at the Universtiy of Rochester studying Computer Science with a minor in History. I've taken classes in Physics, Astronomy, Writing, and Philosophy in addition to my major requirements in Computer Science and Math. I also have proficient skills in reading and writing Italian and experience with verbal Italian.
I am a brother in the ΔΧ-Delta Chi chapter of ΦΚΤ-Phi Kappa Tau Fraternity at the University of Rochester and am currently serving as the IFC (Inter-Fraternity Council) Representative for my chapter.

I will be leading a workshop section for CSC 171 - Intro to Computer Science course being taught by Professor Pawlicki. I will lead small workshops of students in learning course materials, solidifying concepts, and working through problems outside of the lecture hall.

In addition to the skills listed on the home page, through my coursework I have worked with MIPS Assembly as well as Racket and functional programming through my coursework.

I am an Eagle Scout in Devon Troop 50, BSA and have attained the Vigil Honor in Octoraro Lodge #22, Order of the Arrow (OA), BSA. I served in many leadership positions in Devon 50, my final position before aging out at 18 was Assistant Senior Patrol Leader. In the Order of the Arrow I performed on the ceremonies team, I served as a chairman of several committees, and was elected to serve as the lodge Treasurer for the 2014 term.

The past three summers I have worked at the Boy Scout camp I have attended since 5th grade. The first two summers I worked as OA Camp Chief, coordinating the OA program at Camp Horseshoe and its partner Cub Scout camp across the river, Camp Ware. This past summer I worked as the Archery Director for Camp Horseshoe.

My high school, Conestoga High School in Berwyn, PA, has a program for seniors to intern at a business for the last month of school. During this program we meet with school faculty advisors and our sponsors onsite at the business to discuss what we are learning. I interned at Pietragallo Gordon Alfano Bosick & Raspanti, LLP. I worked under the guidance of Mr. Douglas K. Rosenblum, Esq. I assisted with the general office work and the case work.

From March 2013 until I had to leave for universtiy I was a peer mentor in the Friend Fitness program of the Youth Mentoring Partnership in Great Valley, PA. The program helped youth learn goal-setting, determination, self-worth, and 'grit' through fitness.
